Awesome Features

Enjoy the amazing services provided on the platform

Medical Course Quiz

Test your knowledge of medical courses by participating in timed quizzes organized by groups or individuals. You can also create your on quiz for people to participate.

Medical Community

This platform provide opportunity to join various groups therein, connect with other people and participate in their quizzes. You can also create your own group(s) so other people can join.

Performance Ratings

Each quiz (either by groups or individuals) has a score board for all participants. Doing the same quiz a second time will update your previous score to your new score.

Students Interaction

This platform also provides an avenue to chat with other students one-on-one and interact. contains chat features like emojis, stickers and images.

Activity Logs

Track your recent activities and get notified when a new quiz or group is being created, when others participate in a quiz or when a new user joins the platform.

User Privacy

One-on-one chats and user passwords are encrypted and protected by hashed algorithms.
